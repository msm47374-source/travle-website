Upper Dolpa Expedition

Cross high Himalayan passes to Shey Gompa and the Crystal Mountain in Nepal’s most secluded, Tibetan-influenced landscape.

Overview

Nepal’s Most Authentic Expedition Trek

Upper Dolpa is a rare corner of the Himalaya where high passes, stone villages, and living Tibetan culture come together in a true expedition-style journey. The trek begins with a flight to Juphal and follows remote valleys deep into Shey Phoksundo National Park.

The highlight is reaching Shey Gompa beneath the sacred Crystal Mountain, a pilgrimage site steeped in both Buddhism and the ancient Bon tradition. Expect long walking days, wild camps and simple lodges, and unforgettable crossings of high passes such as Kang La and Jeng La.

Because Upper Dolpa is a restricted region, permits and logistics are essential. We handle the paperwork, staff, camping equipment, and safety protocols so you can focus on the experience.

Day-by-Day

Upper Dolpa Itinerary

This is a classic 24-day outline. Weather and flights can shift days in remote Nepal.

01 Arrival in Kathmandu

Airport pickup, hotel check-in, and a welcome briefing with our team.

02 Permits, gear check & trek briefing

Final preparations, restricted permit verification, and staff coordination.

03 Fly to Nepalgunj

A short flight west to Nepalgunj for an overnight before the mountain flight.

04 Fly to Juphal & trek to Dunai

Scenic mountain flight to Juphal. Trek along river valleys to Dunai, the district hub.

05 Trek to Tarakot

Follow the river gorge past small settlements and terraced fields to Tarakot.

06 Trek to Laini / camp

Transition into wilder terrain as the valley narrows and villages thin out.

07 Trek to Nawarpani / camp

A steady day through remote gorges and side streams, camping in a broad valley.

08 Trek to Dho Tarap

Enter the high plateau environment and reach Dho Tarap, a major cultural village.

09 Acclimatization day in Dho Tarap

Short hikes and cultural exploration to help the body adapt safely.

10 Trek to Numala Base Camp

An expedition-style day heading toward the first major pass.

11 Cross Numala Pass (5,190m) to camp

Long, demanding day across a high pass with wide mountain views and a remote camp.

12 Cross Baga La Pass (5,170m) to Ringmo area

Another dramatic pass day, descending toward the Phoksundo valley region.

13 Trek to Phoksundo area (itinerary dependent)

Depending on route choice and conditions, visit viewpoints and lakeside settlements.

14 Trek toward Shey Gompa

Move deeper into Upper Dolpa with a higher, drier, Tibetan plateau feel.

15 Cross Kang La (5,360m) to Shey Gompa

Signature day: cross Kang La to the sacred Shey Gompa below Crystal Mountain.

16 Exploration day: Shey Gompa & Crystal Mountain

A full day for monastery visits, local interaction, and optional hikes.

17 Trek to Namgung / Saldang area

Traverse high plateaus to villages known for Tibetan culture and trading routes.

18 Trek deeper into Saldang valley

Explore a chain of stone villages with stunning valley scenery and prayer walls.

19 Cross Jeng La (5,090m) toward Dho Tarap

Final major pass crossing before rejoining the main return route.

20 Trek to Dho Tarap (return)

Return to familiar villages, with time for rest and cultural visits.

21 Trek to Tarakot

Descend through valleys and river crossings, easing back into greener landscapes.

22 Trek to Dunai

Final walking days with team celebration and preparation for the flight out.

23 Trek to Juphal & fly to Nepalgunj

Trek to the airstrip and fly back to Nepalgunj (weather permitting).

24 Fly to Kathmandu & departure planning

Return to Kathmandu for a farewell dinner and onward travel support.
