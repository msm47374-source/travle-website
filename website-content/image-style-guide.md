# Image Style Guide - Biraj Oli Cleaning (Darwin)

Use this guide for all AI-generated website images so visuals feel cohesive across homepage, service pages, and ads.

## Visual Direction

- **Style Goal**: Premium, trustworthy, local, and practical.
- **Mood**: Bright, clean, confident, and welcoming.
- **Look**: Real-world documentary-cleaning style (not overly staged).
- **Consistency Rule**: Keep framing, color grade, and lighting uniform across all image sets.

## Core Camera Profile

| Element | Standard |
|---|---|
| Camera Model | Sony A7 IV |
| Primary Lens | 24-70mm f/2.8 |
| Secondary Lens | 35mm f/1.8 (for tighter human-focused shots) |
| Focal Length | 28-35mm for interiors, 50mm for staff portraits, 24mm for wide exterior scenes |
| Aperture | f/4 for service scenes, f/2.8-f/3.2 for portrait emphasis |
| Shutter | 1/125 to 1/250 for handheld clarity |
| ISO | 100-400 daylight, 400-800 low light interiors |
| White Balance | 5200K daylight baseline, adjust slightly warm if space feels sterile |

## Lighting Style

- Use soft natural daylight where possible.
- Simulate window-side key light plus subtle bounce fill.
- Avoid harsh flash, crushed shadows, and over-processed HDR.
- Keep highlight roll-off gentle on reflective surfaces (glass, tiles, steel).

## Color Tone and Grade

- **Tone**: Neutral-clean with mild warm highlights.
- **Palette**: Whites, soft greys, navy accents, fresh green touches.
- **Saturation**: Moderate, not hyper-vivid.
- **Contrast**: Medium for detail in tiles, carpets, and uniforms.
- **Skin Tones**: Natural and realistic.

## Composition Rules

- Prefer horizontal 16:9 for hero banners and section headers.
- Keep vertical lines straight for architectural and interior shots.
- Show active cleaning action, not just empty rooms.
- Include human presence in at least 60% of service-page images.
- Leave negative space for text overlays in hero images.

## Setting Descriptions for Generation

### Residential Scenes

- Modern Darwin home interiors with natural light, fans, tiled floors, tidy furniture.
- Cleaner in branded navy polo, gloves, and professional tools visible.
- Before/after style framing with same camera position for comparison sets.

### Commercial Scenes

- Offices, retail counters, corridors, and glass entries in Darwin-like tropical urban context.
- Early-morning or after-hours cleaning activity with visible hygiene process.
- Clean uniforms, clear PPE where relevant, realistic equipment scale.

### Specialist and High-Risk Scenes

- Controlled, professional environment with correct PPE and safety posture.
- Emphasize process and compliance visuals over dramatic effects.
- Avoid graphic or distressing details for public-facing pages.

### Outdoor and Exterior Scenes

- Driveways, facades, patios, and pathways under soft daylight.
- Tropical Darwin cues: bright sky, heat-soft shadows, lush greenery.
- Keep water spray and pressure-cleaning effects realistic and directional.

## Prompt Structure Template

Use this base structure for AI image prompts:

```text
Professional cleaning scene in Darwin, Australia, documentary commercial photography, Sony A7 IV, 24-70mm lens at 32mm, f/4, soft natural daylight with bounce fill, neutral-clean color grade with mild warm highlights, realistic uniforms and equipment, sharp detail, straight architectural lines, clean modern environment, negative space for website text overlay, high realism.
```

## Do and Do Not

- **Do**: Keep people, tools, and surfaces realistic and proportional.
- **Do**: Match lighting direction across image batches.
- **Do**: Keep branding colors subtle and consistent.
- **Do Not**: Use cartoonish styling, extreme blur, or artificial skin smoothing.
- **Do Not**: Overuse dramatic lens flare or unreal reflections.
- **Do Not**: Create misleading safety or PPE depictions.
