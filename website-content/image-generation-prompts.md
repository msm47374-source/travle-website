## Negative Prompt

Apply this to all images where supported:

```text
cartoon, illustration, painting, anime, CGI, plastic skin, extra fingers, distorted hands, duplicate tools, unrealistic reflections, extreme lens flare, text, watermark, logo, dark moody lighting, oversaturated color, unsafe PPE, graphic trauma detail
```
