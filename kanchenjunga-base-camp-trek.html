Overview

A True Wilderness Expedition

Kanchenjunga is the trek for travelers who want Nepal's raw, untamed side. Far from the busy trails, the route moves through remote villages, deep river valleys, and high alpine terrain inside the Kanchenjunga Conservation Area.

This classic base camp itinerary is designed for steady acclimatization and includes cultural encounters with local communities of eastern Nepal. The reward is reaching viewpoints and base camp areas beneath the 'Five Treasures of the Snow' - the mighty peaks of Kanchenjunga (8,586m).

Expect long walking days, basic lodges, and a genuine sense of isolation - with full support from our local guiding team and logistics staff.

Day-by-Day

22-Day Itinerary

This is a classic outline. Road/flight and weather can adjust the schedule.

01 Arrival in Kathmandu

Airport pickup, hotel check-in, and briefing.

02 Permits & trek preparation

Gear check, permits processing, and final logistics.

03 Travel to eastern Nepal (flight/drive)

Transfer toward the trek start area (route varies by season/conditions).

04 Trek start: villages & terraced hills

Begin trekking through foothills and local settlements.

05 Trek deeper into the valley

Forest trails and river crossings as you move away from roads.

06 Trek to higher settlements

Climb steadily, gaining altitude with scenic ridge views.

07 Trek through rhododendron forests

A signature day in spring, with bloom-filled forest sections.

08 Trek to alpine zone

Terrain opens up with bigger mountain views and cooler air.

09 Acclimatization / exploration day

Rest and short hikes for safe adaptation.

10 Trek toward base camp valley

Remote high valley trekking as glaciers appear nearby.

11 Base camp approach day

Moraines and alpine terrain lead to the base camp area.

12 Kanchenjunga Base Camp viewpoint

The reward day - panoramic views of the Kanchenjunga massif (weather permitting).

13-20 Return trek via alternate villages

Retrace and vary the route back with cultural stops and gradual descent.

21 Travel back to Kathmandu

Drive/flight back to Kathmandu, farewell dinner.

22 Departure

Airport transfer and onward travel support.
