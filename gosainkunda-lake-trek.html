Gosainkunda Lake Trek

A spiritual pilgrimage to high-altitude holy lakes, surrounded by the majestic peaks of the Langtang and Ganesh Himal.

Overview

The Sacred Alpine Sanctuary

The Gosainkunda Lake Trek is one of Nepal's most revered spiritual and natural journeys. Located within the Langtang National Park, this trek takes you to the holy Gosainkunda Lake (4,380m), a site of immense religious significance for both Hindus and Buddhists. According to Hindu mythology, Lord Shiva created the lake by striking his trident into the mountain to quench his thirst.

The trail begins with a drive to Dhunche and ascends through lush rhododendron and oak forests, home to the elusive Red Panda. As you climb higher, the landscape transitions into rugged alpine terrain, offering spectacular panoramas of the Ganesh Himal, Langtang Lirung, and the Manaslu range.

The highlight of the trek is the crystal-clear waters of the sacred lake itself, often surrounded by several other smaller high-altitude lakes like Bhairab Kunda and Saraswati Kunda. Whether you seek spiritual peace or high-altitude adventure, Gosainkunda delivers an unforgettable Himalayan experience.

Highlights

Why You'll Love This Trek

  • Sacred Gosainkunda Lake

    Experience the serene, deep blue waters of the holy lake, a major pilgrimage site for Hindus and Buddhists.

  • Panoramic Views

    Enjoy breathtaking vistas of Langtang Lirung, Ganesh Himal, Manaslu, and the Annapurna range from Lauribinayak.

  • Shin Gompa Monastery

    Visit the peaceful Buddhist monastery and taste the famous local yak cheese at the nearby factory.

  • Rare Wildlife

    Trek through the dense forests of Langtang National Park, habitat of the rare and elusive Red Panda.

  • Tamang Culture

    Engage with the hospitable Tamang people and learn about their unique high-altitude lifestyle and traditions.

  • Rhododendron Forests

    Walk through vibrant forests that come alive with red, pink, and white blooms during the spring season.

  • Bhairab & Saraswati Kunda

    Explore the smaller adjoining lakes, each with its own spiritual significance and natural beauty.

  • Lauribinayak Pass (4,610m)

    Opt for the challenging climb to the pass for even more expansive views across the Himalayas.

  • Accessible Adventure

    One of the most accessible high-altitude treks from Kathmandu, perfect for those with limited time.

Day-by-Day

Detailed Itinerary

8 days to the sacred lakes — click any day to expand

01 Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you will be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Evening briefing about the trek and a welcome dinner. Overnight in Kathmandu.

02 Drive Kathmandu → Dhunche (1,960m)

A scenic drive of 7-8 hours takes us through the winding roads of hills and valleys to Dhunche, the administrative headquarters of the Rasuwa district. The journey offers glimpses of local life and terraced hillsides. Overnight in Dhunche.

03 Dhunche → Shin Gompa (3,330m)

The trek begins with a steady ascent through forests of oak, pine, and rhododendron. We pass through Deurali (2,625m) before reaching the peaceful village of Shin Gompa. Visit the local cheese factory and the old monastery. Walking time: 5-6 hours.

04 Shin Gompa → Gosainkunda (4,380m)

A more challenging day with significant altitude gain. The ascent leads to Lauribinayak (3,910m), offering stunning views of the Ganesh Himal and Langtang Lirung. Continuing upward, we reach the sacred Gosainkunda Lake. Walking time: 5-6 hours.

05 Explore Gosainkunda → Shin Gompa (3,330m)

Early morning exploration of the lake and its surroundings. You can hike to nearby viewpoints for sunrise. After breakfast, we begin our descent back toward Shin Gompa, retracing our steps with magnificent mountain views. Walking time: 5-6 hours.

06 Shin Gompa → Dhunche (1,960m)

We continue our descent through the lush forests down to Dhunche. It's a pleasant walk as the air becomes warmer and richer in oxygen. Enjoy a farewell dinner with the trekking crew. Walking time: 4-5 hours.

07 Drive Dhunche → Kathmandu (1,400m)

After breakfast, we board our vehicle for the drive back to Kathmandu. Upon arrival, you'll be transferred to your hotel. The rest of the day is free for shopping or relaxation in Thamel. Overnight in Kathmandu.

08 Final Departure

Our representative will transfer you to the international airport for your flight back home or to your next destination. Namaste!
