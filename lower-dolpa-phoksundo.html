The Jewel of Shey Phoksundo

This 14-day journey is the perfect introduction to Dolpa � remote enough to feel wild, but shorter and more accessible than Upper Dolpa. After flying to Juphal, you trek through deep valleys and pine forests into Shey Phoksundo National Park.

The highlight is reaching the mesmerizing Phoksundo Lake (3,611m), known for its intense turquoise color. You�ll also visit the traditional village of Ringmo and see one of Nepal�s highest waterfalls. This trek blends nature, culture, and comfort, with lodges on most nights.

Lower Dolpa is ideal for trekkers who want a remote experience without long expedition logistics. Our pacing focuses on safety, enjoyment, and authentic cultural encounters.

Day-by-Day

14-Day Itinerary

A classic Lower Dolpa route to Phoksundo (minor changes may occur due to flights/weather).

01 Arrival in Kathmandu

Airport pickup, hotel check-in, and briefing.

02 Fly to Nepalgunj

Fly west to Nepalgunj and prepare for the mountain flight.

03 Fly to Juphal & trek to Dunai

Scenic flight to Juphal, then an easy trek to Dunai.

04 Trek to Chhepka

Follow the river valley, crossing bridges and entering forested trails.

05 Trek to Jharana / waterfall area

A beautiful day to the Phoksundo waterfall viewpoint region.

06 Trek to Ringmo & Phoksundo Lake

Reach Ringmo village and enjoy your first views of turquoise Phoksundo.

07 Acclimatization / exploration day at the lake

Explore the lakeshore, viewpoints, and local monasteries.

08 Optional day hike (route dependent)

Optional hikes for photography and culture, based on conditions and interest.

09 Trek back toward Chhepka

Begin the return journey through forests and river valleys.

10 Trek to Dunai

Descend to Dunai with time to relax and celebrate with the team.

11 Trek to Juphal

Final trek day to Juphal for the morning flight.

12 Fly to Nepalgunj

Fly out of the mountains (weather permitting) and overnight in Nepalgunj.

13 Fly to Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu, free time for shopping/rest.

14 Departure

Airport transfer and onward travel assistance.
