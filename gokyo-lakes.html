Overview

A Journey to the Emerald Lakes

The Gokyo Lakes trek is widely considered the most stunning alternative to the classic Everest Base Camp route. It leads you into the heart of the Khumbu through a different valley, leading to a series of six spectacular turquoise glacial lakes.

Beyond the mesmerizing lakes, the trek offers a climb to the summit of Gokyo Ri (5,357m). From here, you'll witness a vista that many argue surpasses that of Kala Patthar, featuring four of the world's fourteen 8,000m peaks: Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu.

Traversing the Ngozumpa Glacier—the largest in the Nepal Himalayas—and staying in the high-altitude settlement of Gokyo, this trek is a perfect blend of natural wonder and cultural depth.

Daily Route

14 Days Itinerary

01 Arrival in Kathmandu

Transfer to hotel and trip briefing.

02 Fly to Lukla & Trek to Phakding

35 min flight followed by a 3-4 hour trek (2,610m).

03 Trek to Namche Bazaar

Steep climb to the Sherpa capital (3,440m).

04 Acclimatization in Namche

Hike to Everest View Hotel for first glimpses of the giants.

05 Trek to Phortse Thanga

Branching off toward the Gokyo Valley (3,680m).

06 Trek to Machhermo

Steady climb through rhododendron forests (4,470m).

07 Trek to Gokyo

Witnessing the first and second lakes before arriving at Gokyo (4,800m).

08 Explore Fourth & Fifth Lakes

A stunning day trip further up the valley toward Cho Oyu base.

09 Gokyo Ri Hike & Trek to Dole

Sunrise hike to Gokyo Ri (5,357m) then descent to Dole (4,200m).

10 Trek to Namche Bazaar

Retracing steps back to the bustling hub.

11 Trek to Lukla

Final trekking day back to the airstrip.

12 Fly to Kathmandu

Return flight and free afternoon in Thamel.

13 Free Day in Kathmandu

Shopping and optional sightseeing.

14 Departure

Transfer to airport for your flight home.
