Overview

The Full Kanchenjunga Circuit

Click any day to expand the detailed route information

The Kanchenjunga Circuit Trek is the complete eastern Nepal expedition, built for experienced trekkers seeking longer days, higher terrain, and very remote trail systems.

You move from subtropical villages to alpine zones, then onward into glacier-fed valleys and high passes with prolonged views of the Kanchenjunga massif.

Compared with base camp-only routes, this circuit adds route variety, deeper cultural encounters, and a stronger expedition atmosphere while maintaining careful acclimatization pacing.

Common Questions

Kanchenjunga Circuit FAQ

Click any day to expand the detailed route information

Is this harder than the Kanchenjunga base camp route?
Yes. The circuit is generally longer and more remote, with sustained high-altitude days and fewer facilities.
Do we need camping support for this circuit?
Some departures use mixed lodge-and-camp logistics based on conditions and exact route selection. We confirm this before departure.
What is the best season for stable weather?
Spring (April-May) and autumn (October-November) are best for visibility and trail conditions.
